About us

About us

Fave Pictures is a subsidiary of Midas Touch Business Emporium Limited. The company was registered in 2018 but commenced operations early 2019.

Fave Pictures is a state of-the-art professional photography studio for Children, teen, adult and families in Abuja,Nigeria. We are creatives and artists with a real heart for family. We want to inspire and influence generations of families on the importance of photo archiving and sharing their best moments together. We are a passionate company with vision to encourage people to realize the importance of family and the blessings that children bring even from the early stages of life. We are proud of what we have built and hope we can inspire families with lasting and unforgettable memories.

Vision

To be the leading family-centric photo studio that capture and document life’s most unforgettable moment and inspire families to share their best moments together.

Mission

To create, capture and document unforgettable moments of your life with warmth & artistry and help preserve them as lifetime memories.

Core values

  • Godliness & Integrity
  • Creativity & Excellence
  • Diligence & Consistency

Management

Fave Pictures is managed by very experienced professionals trained locally and internationally in photography, editing, studio management and customer service.

Staff

Fave Pictures is staffed by dedicated professionals who are experts in their various fields of endeavors – from photographers, to graphic artist to customer service officials etc. Our staff are well-dressed, courteous, presentable and friendly with outstanding people’s skills. They are proud of their jobs and treat our clients with respect and honor. This explains why at Fave Pictures, our clients become friends and family after their first visit.

BIO
